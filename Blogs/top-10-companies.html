What to Look for in an Interior Design Company

Before diving into the list, it's important to understand what separates a great interior design firm from an average one. Nepal's market has grown quickly, and not all studios deliver equally. Here are the key factors we evaluated:

  • Portfolio breadth — Can they handle residential, commercial, and hospitality projects?
  • Material partnerships — Do they work with premium brands like Hafele, Kohler, or Hettich?
  • Turnkey capability — Can they manage design AND construction under one roof?
  • Local expertise — Do they understand Nepal's climate, seismic codes, and regulations?
  • Client reviews — What do real clients say on Google and social media?
  • On-time delivery — Is there a track record of meeting deadlines?

"The best interior design company is not necessarily the biggest — it's the one that listens most carefully and delivers your vision most faithfully."

— Industry Insight, Nepal Design Association

How to Choose the Right Design Firm

With so many talented firms available, the right choice depends on your project type, budget, and personal style. Here's a quick framework:

  • For hotel or large hospitality projects — choose a firm with proven turnkey delivery and hospitality experience. NextGen Interiors is the clear leader here.
  • For luxury residential — prioritise firms with strong material partnerships and 3D visualization capabilities.
  • For commercial offices — look for ergonomic expertise and corporate project references.
  • For tight budgets — be upfront about your budget from day one; many firms can work within constraints if communicated early.
  • For architecture + interiors together — choose a firm offering both under one roof to avoid coordination headaches.

"A successful interior design project begins with choosing the right partner. Take time to review portfolios, check references, and trust your gut when you meet the team."

— NextGen Interiors, Kathmandu
