Our Story

Born From a
Passion for Design

NextGen Interiors was founded with a singular vision: to bring world-class interior design and architecture to Nepal. What started as a small studio in Kathmandu has grown into one of the most trusted design firms in the region.

We believe that every space tells a story. Our team works closely with each client to understand their vision, lifestyle, and aspirations — then crafts environments that not only look beautiful, but feel like home.

From luxury residences to five-star hotels, from commercial offices to landscaped courtyards, we approach every project with the same dedication to quality, detail, and client satisfaction.

Our Approach

Collaborative Design,
Exceptional Results

What began as a vision to deliver high-quality wooden craftsmanship has today evolved into a comprehensive interior and architectural solution. During my journey in this industry, I observed two critical gaps — lack of proper project coordination and inconsistent material quality. These challenges often led clients to confusion, delays, and compromised outcomes. This realization became the foundation of NextGen Interiors & Architect.

We are built on a simple belief — every space deserves thoughtful design, the right materials, and flawless execution. Not all materials are equal, and not every design fits every need. Our role is to bridge that gap with expertise, clarity, and trust. From a humble beginning, we have grown by focusing on strong relationships, attention to detail, and a commitment to excellence in every project we undertake. Today, NextGen stands as a partner — not just a service provider — helping clients transform their vision into meaningful spaces.
